This version uses 12 broad axes, simpler wording, and one question per screen so it feels cleaner and less repetitive. Questions focus on tradeoffs instead of mirrored opposites.

How it works

You answer with five buttons: two disagree options, neutral, and two agree options. Each answer shifts one axis. At the end you get:

• a political compass
• all 12 axis sliders
• your closest ideology
• your top 10 ideology matches

Compass uses Economics on the horizontal axis and Authority/Liberty on the vertical axis.
